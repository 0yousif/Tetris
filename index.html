Tetris Rules

Blocks fall from the top - your job is to fit them together neatly. When you fill a complete row, it disappears and you earn points. The more lines you clear at once, the higher your score!
Controls:
◄ ► = Move left/right
▲ = Rotate
▼ = Drop faster

Game Over when blocks stack to the top.
Tip: Plan ahead and leave space for long pieces!
